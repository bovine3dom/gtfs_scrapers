DFDS Ferry Timetables

Timetable information

  • Your booking confirmation always states times of check-in, departure, and arrival.
  • You will need the booking number at the check-in desks or self-service check-in machines in the terminal.
  • Your booking number can be found in the booking confirmation.
  • All times mentioned are in local time.

See more travel information and see where to find the terminals.

PLEASE NOTE:
Dieppe-Newhaven: Times may vary depending on the tides.
Baltic routes: Tickets online can be purchased until 5 hours before departure.

DFDS ferry mobile app

The DFDS app is your ideal travel companion.

  • View live departures and arrival times
  • Access your booking pass
  • Keep up to date with the latest travel announcements
  • Follow your journey with your personalised itinerary
  • Find information about onboard facilities
  • Find directions to your DFDS terminals

Simply log into the app using your booking number to access these great features.
